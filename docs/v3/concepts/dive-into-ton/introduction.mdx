# The Open Network

**The Open Network (TON)** is a decentralized platform that forms the foundation for the open internet. It integrates several core components— _TON Blockchain, TON DNS, TON Storage, TON Sites, and TON Proxy_ into a unified ecosystem.

At its core is **TON Blockchain**,
a scalable and secure protocol designed to enable seamless cross-chain interoperability.
It supports high-throughput processing,
aiming to handle millions of transactions per second and serve hundreds of millions of users.

Functioning as a decentralized supercomputer,
TON Blockchain powers the services and applications that bring the vision of an open, user-owned internet to life.
