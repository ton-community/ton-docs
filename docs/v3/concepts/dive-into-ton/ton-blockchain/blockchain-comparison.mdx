# Blockchain comparison

This page provides a quick comparison of TON, Ethereum, and Solana.

|                            | Ethereum   | Solana      | TON         |
|----------------------------|------------|-------------|-------------|
| **Consensus**              | Proof of Stake | Proof of History | Proof‑of‑stake (Catchain)    |
| **TPS**                    | 100,000 TPS | 59,400 TPS  | 104,715 TPS |
| **Block time**             | 12 seconds | &lt; 1 second | &lt; 1 second |
| **Time-to-finality**       | 10-15 minutes | ~13 seconds | &lt; 3 seconds |
