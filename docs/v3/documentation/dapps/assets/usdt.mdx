## Advantages of USD₮ on TON

### Seamless Telegram integration

[USD₮ on TON](https://ton.org/borderless) integrates with Telegram to provide a user-friendly experience for USD₮ transfers and DeFi.

### Lower transaction fees

Fees for Ethereum USD₮ transfers are calculated dynamically depending on network load. This is why transactions can become expensive.

```text
transaction_fee = gas_used * gas_price
```

- `gas_used` is the amount of gas used during transaction execution.
- `gas_price` is the cost of one unit of gas in Gwei, calculated dynamically.

On the other hand, on the TON Blockchain, USD₮ transfer fees are low; see the [average transaction cost](/v3/documentation/smart-contracts/transaction-fees/fees#average-transaction-cost).

### Fast and scalable

TON’s high throughput and rapid confirmation times enable fast USD₮ transactions.

## Advanced details

:::caution IMPORTANT
On the TON Blockchain, multiple jettons can share the same name; a counterfeit jetton may look identical to the real USD₮ but has no value. Verify legitimacy by confirming the jetton master address.
